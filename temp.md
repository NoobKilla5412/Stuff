## Def $\parallel$ gram

A quadrilateral is a parallelogram  
iff  
both pairs of opposite sides are $\parallel$

## Opp sides $\cong$

G: $ABCD$ is a $\parallel$ gram

P: $\overline{AB}\cong\overline{CD}$  
   $\overline{BC}\cong\overline{AD}$
